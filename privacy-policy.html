Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Who we are

SDWH Limited is a professional services practice operated by Matthew Rees. The website is sdwh.co.uk. For data protection purposes, Matthew Rees is the data controller. Contact: support@sdwh.co.uk

What data we collect

When you contact us through the website or book a consultation, we collect the information you provide — typically your name, email address, organisation and the nature of your enquiry. We do not collect any data automatically beyond standard website analytics.

How we use your data

We use your contact information solely to respond to your enquiry or arrange a consultation. We do not use your data for marketing without your explicit consent. We do not sell, share or transfer your personal data to third parties.

How long we keep your data

We retain contact and enquiry records for two years from the date of last contact, after which they are deleted.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to access, correct, delete or restrict the personal data we hold about you. To exercise any of these rights, contact mr@sdwh.co.uk. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office at ico.org.uk.

International transfers

SDWH Limited operates across the UK and EEA. Any personal data you provide may be accessed from the EEA, which the UK has designated as providing adequate data protection.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy periodically. The current version will always be published at sdwh.co.uk/privacy-policy.
