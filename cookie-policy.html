What are cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the site function and provide information about how it is used.

Cookies on this site

This website is hosted on GitHub Pages via Cloudflare. Essential cookies are required for the site to function. Analytics cookies help us understand how visitors use the site. We do not place any additional cookies ourselves.

Essential cookies cannot be disabled as the site will not function without them. Analytics cookies can be declined using the cookie consent banner when you first visit the site.

Third party cookies

We do not embed social media feeds or advertising that would place additional cookies on your device.
