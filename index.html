Independent economic analysis on complex public sector decisions.

SDWH Limited combines expertise in business, finance and government that is rarely found in one place, providing objective, forward-looking analysis and insight that generates real impact. The analysis draws on economic and regulatory frameworks including competition, market design, risk allocation and cost-benefit analysis, applied to decisions with measurable financial and public interest consequences. These decisions sit at the heart of economic growth strategy across advanced economies. In an era of geopolitical uncertainty and supply chain fragmentation, governments are making active choices about which sectors to support, how to mobilise public and private investment, and how to create the conditions in which scalable businesses can grow and remain rooted in their home markets. SDWH provides analytical insight for both sides of those decisions.
