PAC Hearing Forecast

Predicted Public Accounts Committee oral evidence sessions based on the current NAO work-in-progress list and historical WIP-to-hearing timescale data.

Updated 14 June 2026
NAO WIP reports tracked 28
Median WIP → Oral interval 136 days
Baseline hearing probability 45.7%
WIP Publication Window — expected NAO report publication quarter
Median interval — 136-day benchmark from WIP entry to PAC hearing
Predicted PAC Hearing Range — forecast hearing window with confidence %
Red line — today’s date

Methodology: PAC hearing dates are forecast by applying the historical median interval of 136 days between a report’s entry on the NAO work-in-progress (WIP) list and the corresponding PAC oral evidence session. Confidence percentages are derived from the empirical distribution of WIP-to-hearing intervals observed across all tracked reports. A baseline hearing probability of 45.7% reflects the proportion of WIP reports that proceed to a PAC hearing within the forecast window. This forecast is updated when the NAO WIP list changes.
